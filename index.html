Got Questions?

Everything you need to know before you register.

How do I register for DevConf 2026?

Click "Register Now" at the top of the page, choose your ticket tier (Standard, Pro or Team) and complete checkout. You'll get a confirmation email with your badge QR code instantly.

What is the refund and cancellation policy?

Full refunds are available up to 30 days before the event. After that, tickets can be transferred to a colleague free of charge, but are non-refundable within the final 2 weeks.

Will I get access to session recordings?

Standard passes include 30 days of digital recording access. Pro passes unlock lifetime access to every keynote, talk and workshop recording, including bonus speaker Q&As.

Is there parking or travel support at the venue?

Yes, discounted on-site parking is available for badge holders, and we partner with local hotels for a reduced group rate — details are sent in your confirmation email.

Do you offer group or team discounts?

Absolutely. Our Team pass covers up to 10 people at a flat rate, and larger groups can contact us directly for custom pricing and a dedicated onboarding session.

Is there a dress code or should I bring a laptop?

Come as you are — smart casual or full hoodie mode, both work. A laptop is recommended for workshop days if you want to follow along with hands-on labs.
